About Us

We are a Christ-centered church committed to building lives and communities on the foundation of God’s Word. Our passion is to raise disciples who live with purpose, walk in love, and impact society with kingdom values. Through worship, discipleship, outreach, and service, we shine as light to the nations and bring transformation to generations.

Our Vision

To see lives transformed and nations discipled through the power of the Gospel.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, disciple believers, and raise kingdom-minded leaders for Christ.

Our Core Values

Faith, Love, Service, Integrity, and Excellence guide everything we do.

Our Services

Sunday Worship Service

Our Sunday worship service is the heart of our weekly gathering. Every Sunday, we come together as a family of believers to glorify God through heartfelt worship, prayer, and the teaching of God’s Word. Our services are designed to inspire, encourage, and strengthen your walk with Christ, leaving you renewed and empowered for the week ahead. We believe that worship is more than music—it is an expression of love and devotion to our Savior. From the choir to the congregation, every voice is lifted to honor God. The preaching is Christ-centered, practical, and Spirit-led, ensuring that the message is not only heard but applied in daily living.

Midweek Bible Study

The Midweek Bible Study provides a deeper opportunity for learning, reflection, and growth. It is not just a service but an interactive experience where the Word of God is broken down in practical ways. Here, members are encouraged to ask questions, share insights, and discover new depths in Scripture. Our study sessions cover various themes such as Christian living, faith in action, leadership, family, and spiritual growth. Every session is designed to challenge the mind and nurture the soul, ensuring that every believer is equipped with sound doctrine and biblical wisdom. We believe in Acts 17:11, that believers should "search the Scriptures daily," and our Bible study creates the right atmosphere for this.

Youth and Children’s Ministry

At our church, we believe in raising the next generation with the Word of God. Our Youth and Children’s Ministry provides a safe, nurturing, and exciting environment for young people to grow in faith. Through engaging activities, age-appropriate teachings, mentorship, and fun fellowship, we help children and youth build a strong foundation in Christ. Our dedicated teachers and youth leaders are passionate about helping young people understand their identity in Christ and equipping them to face the challenges of life with courage and wisdom. We also host regular youth-focused events, leadership programs, and creative outreaches that inspire them to use their gifts and talents for God’s glory.

Community Outreach & Evangelism

Our church is deeply committed to serving our community through outreach and evangelism. We believe that the church is called to be the salt of the earth and the light of the world. Through various outreach programs, we extend the love of Christ to the less privileged, the needy, and those who have yet to know Him. Our initiatives include feeding programs, free medical outreach, prison ministry, and evangelistic crusades. We believe that evangelism is not optional but the heartbeat of God, and we actively seek to share the Gospel both locally and globally. Each outreach is carried out with compassion, excellence, and a strong commitment to making disciples of all nations. We are not only a worshiping church but also a serving and sending church.

Our Sermons

Walking in the Light

Preached on August 10, 2025

This sermon reminds us of the eternal truth from 1 John 1:5 — “God is light, in Him there is no darkness at all.” To walk in the light is to walk in truth, love, and integrity. Pastor taught that light exposes, heals, and guides, and we must let God’s light shine through us in all areas of life. We are called to reflect Christ’s light in our families, workplaces, schools, and communities. When believers unite, their lights become a beacon that cannot be hidden. The message ended with a charge: examine your life and let go of anything that dims your light, while staying rooted in prayer and the Word.

Faith that Moves Mountains

Preached on August 17, 2025

In this sermon, Pastor emphasized the power of faith rooted in Christ. Drawing from Matthew 17:20, he explained that true faith is not about the size of our belief but the size of our God. Faith grows through obedience, prayer, and trust in God’s promises. When life presents “mountains” of fear, failure, or delay, faith speaks boldly that nothing is impossible with God. Pastor also reminded the church that faith is not passive — it must be active, accompanied by works of love, patience, and perseverance. The congregation was encouraged to step into new seasons with confidence, declaring God’s word over their situations and expecting supernatural breakthroughs.
